About 🙋

As-salamu alaykum by the Team of Deen - The Way of Life. Our purpose is to promote and spread the teachings of the Holy Quran and Sunnah for the sake of Allah. As Quran states, “Let there be a group among you who call others to good, and enjoin what is right, and forbid what is wrong: those who do this shall be successful.” (Soorat Aal ‛Imraan,3:104)

Note: The Team of Deen - The Way Of Life respects every Sect, believes in the teachings of those Sects which are according to the Quran and Sunnah.
